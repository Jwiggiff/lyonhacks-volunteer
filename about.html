About The Team

We are a group of grade 12 students in Toronto who ran into this idea during a hackathon. Volunteering is incredibly beneficial to everyone, whether it be high school students trying to get involved in the community or an adult wanting to give back. However, we noticed that there really isn’t a single go-to place to find opportunities, and to post them. And that’s how :Insert name here: was born.
