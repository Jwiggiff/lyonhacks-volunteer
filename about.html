About The Team

We are a group of grade 12 students in Toronto who ran into this idea during a hackathon. Volunteering is incredibly beneficial to everyone, whether it be a high school student trying to get involved in the community or an adult wanting to give back. However, we noticed that there really isn’t a single go-to place to find opportunities, and to post them. And that’s how Volunteerism was born.
